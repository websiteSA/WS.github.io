About

Material handling is a basic requirement in all types of industrial operation. Critical equipment like crane, mobile equipment and locomotives are engaged for material handling. The operational safety and reliability of these equipment depend upon quality inspection and effective health assessment. Due to lack of knowledge and competency in this field, lot of unsafe incidences with severe consequence take place resulting in loss of life and property damage. We serve the equipment suppliers with our expertise to deliver healthy equipment to their customers.

Executive Summary

Services

Equipment Inspection Services (hereinafter called EIS) are engaged ininspection and health assessment of all types of cranes, mobile equipment and locomotives deployed in steel plant, mines & collieries, cement plant, fertilizer plant, port trusts, power plant, electricity boards and various other engineering and process industries of India and abroad.
